This campaign begins in the peaceful village of [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]], with a nearby ruin, [[Wraithmoor Keep\|Wraithmoor Keep]], holding dark secrets. Villagers have gone missing over the years and recently started returning—broken and seemingly cursed.  
  
---  
The adventure begins on a foggy morning in the village square. The party is visiting [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]] when the town bell tolls urgently, summoning the villagers.  
  
The mayor, Edric Vane, announces grim news:  
> "They found another one. Poor Maren... gone three years now... and back, but she's not the same."  
  
Recently, villagers who vanished near [[Wraithmoor Keep\|Wraithmoor Keep]] have begun returning. They are pale, gaunt, and silent—hollow, fragmented, and unable to recall what happened.
