The city of [[Places/Draughelm\|Draughelm]], once prosperous, now festers with unease. Beneath its streets lies a terrible truth: the ancient titan skeleton [[Skel'zaroth\|Skel'zaroth]] secretly rules through fear and manipulation.
Its influence spreads through a cult known as the [[Silent Hand\|Silent Hand]], whose members infiltrate every level of society.

---
The party arrives in [[Places/Draughelm\|Draughelm]], a bustling but tense city. Rumors spread of disappearances, strange behavior among guards, and whispers of a hidden cult.

# Rewards
## Magic User
- [[Custom Creations/Custom Items\|Amulet of Arcane Restoration]]
	- A silver amulet with a gemstone that pulses with arcane energy. When worn, it restores 1 spell slot of 3rd level or lower after a short rest. Additionally, once per day, the wearer can use Counterspell without expending a spell slot.
## Elf
- [[Custom Creations/Custom Items\|Bow of the Ancient Grove]]
	- An elegant longbow crafted from the wood of a sacred elven tree. Arrows shot from this bow are imbued with the power of nature. They deal an additional 1d6 force damage and return to the quiver after being fired. Once per day, the bow can summon an Ent-like ally to assist the elf for 10 minutes.
## Fighter
- [[Custom Creations/Custom Items\|Gauntlets of Might]]
	- These enchanted gauntlets increase the strength and power of their wearer. They grant a +2 bonus to Strength checks and saving throws. Additionally, once per day, the fighter can activate the gauntlets to double their damage output for one turn, including adding +2 to the damage of any melee attack.
