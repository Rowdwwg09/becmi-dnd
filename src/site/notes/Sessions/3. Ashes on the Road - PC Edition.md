After the fall of [[Skel'zaroth\|Skel'zaroth]], the party escorts [[Important NPC's/Aldrin Farrow\|Aldrin Farrow]] back to [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]] for payment. But the road is not safe—strange shadowy remnants linger in the wilds. Forced off course, the party must travel through haunted ruins, where it becomes clear that the danger did not end in Draughelm.

---
The cold wind of [[Places/Draughelm\|Draughelm]] carries ash and silence. As you recover from your battle, the sound of wheels breaks the stillness.
A carriage—one you recognize—rolls slowly toward you.
The driver’s door creaks open. A hooded figure steps out… and collapses. His cloak withers into ash as it hits the ground.

> “I… I don’t know… where am I?”

The man, **Eryndor Vale**, is disoriented and weak. He insists he must return to [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]].

At the same time, [[Important NPC's/Aldrin Farrow\|Aldrin Farrow]] prepares for the journey, reminding you of the payment waiting there.

The road ahead is uncertain—and dangerous.
