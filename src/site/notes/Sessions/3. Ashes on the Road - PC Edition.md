After the fall of [[Skel'zaroth\|Skel'zaroth]], the party escorts [[Important NPC's/Aldrin Farrow\|Aldrin Farrow]] back to [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]] for payment. But the road is not safe—strange shadowy remnants linger in the wilds. Forced off course, the party must travel through haunted ruins, where it becomes clear that the danger did not end in Draughelm.

---
The cold wind of [[Places/Draughelm\|Draughelm]] carries ash and silence. As you recover from your battle, the sound of wheels breaks the stillness.
A carriage—one you recognize—rolls slowly toward you.
The driver’s door creaks open. A hooded figure steps out… and collapses. His cloak withers into ash as it hits the ground.

> “I… I don’t know… where am I?”

The man, **Eryndor Vale**, is disoriented and weak. He insists he must return to [[Places/Willowshade\|Willowshade]].

At the same time, [[Important NPC's/Aldrin Farrow\|Aldrin Farrow]] prepares for the journey, reminding you of the payment waiting there.

The road ahead is uncertain—and dangerous.

- Race: Human
- Alignment: Neutral
- Background: A scholar who once studied ruins and forbidden knowledge. His memory is fractured, and he speaks in strange fragments.
#### [[Important NPC's/Aldrin Farrow\|Aldrin Farrow]]
- Human Merchant, practical and cautious
> “Coin’s the only loyalty that matters on the road.”

---
#### Shadow Wraithlings
-Thin, flickering shapes drift at the edge of vision, barely more than shadows given form. Their bodies stretch unnaturally, like smoke pulled by an unseen wind, and their hollow eyes glow faintly in the dark. When they move, they don’t walk, they _slide_, phasing between trees and mist as if the world barely holds them.
- Beat it

---
#### Carrion Hounds
- These massive, black-furred beasts prowl low to the ground, their ribs visible beneath taut skin. Their eyes burn with a dull, hungry glow, and their jaws drip with dark saliva. They move in eerie silence for creatures their size, until they strike, erupting into snarling chaos as a pack.
- Beat it

---
#### Shadow-Touched Bandits
- At first glance, they seem like ordinary highwaymen, but something is wrong. Their skin is pale and stretched, their eyes sunken and restless. Shadows cling to them unnaturally, shifting even in steady light. When they fight, desperation twists into something feral, as if another presence is pushing them beyond their limits.
- Beat it

### The Hollow Chapel
Inside, faint light filters through shattered stained glass.
A presence forms near the altar.
> “The master may be ash… but I remain.”

A shadowy figure rises to face you.

### Shade Revenant
 - A towering figure of darkness, its form constantly shifting between solid and smoke. Tattered remnants of armor and cloth cling to its body, as if dragged back from death itself. Its voice echoes unnaturally, layered with whispers, and its presence alone makes the air feel heavy and cold, like something ancient refusing to stay buried.
